End the Oakland Protest Curfew

Mayor Libby Schaaf has started enforcing an old Oakland law that bans people from gathering in the street after dark without a permit. Unsurprisingly, the rule has only been enforced against Black-led protests demanding an end to police violence on Black bodies.

Take some basic online action using the buttons below — and then get out in the streets to protest, because this assault on free speech is unacceptable and only helps maintain a culture of white supremacy.
